Privacy Policy

How we Collect, Use, and Share your data, what we collect, and how to remove it.

When we collect your data and what we collect

We collect some data whenever you use any of our services, this data includes: Email that you share with us, IP address, Time Zone, System Information (which browser, what system, and what screen size). Services that collect data from may include, but are not limited to, AntiNuker, SynrupBot, ClumsyBot, iClient, and Project: Mountain.

How we use your data

We use your data anonymously, nothing is tied to your name (unless you are on our service blacklist which then all of your data is stored to keep you from using our services) to run services for you, services like SynDash or AntiNuker require this data to function, other services like AntiNuker, SynrupBot, or ClumsyBot for feedback data for improvement. All data collected (if not for the blacklist) will be deleted after 30 days.

How to remove your data

To remove your data you must first:
  • Not be in any Blacklist with Synrup Security Agency
  • Be in good standing with SleepyKoder Projects
  • Have an account with us
If you meet all of these requirements you can email us at: sleepykoder@proton.me with the subject "Remove" with the message "Remove User" followed by your email and then your Discord User ID.

When we share your data

We do not share your data to anyone. Nobody can see your IP address.
