Efficiency

Reliability

We hope to have our services with little to no downtime for 2023, so that you can use our services at any point of the day, at any point of the year.

Sustainability

We hope to move all of our servers to using a private set of solar panels and L-ion batteries to power our servers by 2024, all with the earth in mind.
